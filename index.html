I am a Research Fellow at Microsoft Turing, where I work with Dr. Monojit Choudhury, Dr. Subhojit Som, Dr. Nishanth Chandran (Microsoft Research India) and Dr. Saurabh Tiwary (CVP Microsoft Turing).

Currently, I am interested in understanding the limitations and capabilities of contemporary AI systems such as Large Language Models (LLMs) covering various aspects including reasoning and memorisation. My objective is to leverage this understanding to develop effective and interpretable AI systems addressing diverse problems, not only limited to theorem proving, factual question answering, and program synthesis.

Keywords: formal theorem proving, analysis and interpretability, search and game playing

I graduated with B.Tech in Electrical Engineering from IIT Kharagpur, India in 2022. For more details about my background, refer to my CV. If you'd like to chat with me about my work or research in general, feel free to reach out!
